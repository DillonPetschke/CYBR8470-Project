About

Information Technology Professionals Organization – Lincoln

It is the mission of ITPO to provide superior leadership and education in Information Technology. ITPO is dedicated to using the synergy of Information Technology partnerships to provide education and benefits to our members and to working with the industry to assist in the overall promotion and direction of Information Technology.

We hold monthly meetings on the 3rd Wednesday of each month. These meetings cover topics such as Cyber Security, Computer Networking, Programming, business tours, and more! Select meetings are open to the public while ITPO members have access to all meetings throughout the year.
